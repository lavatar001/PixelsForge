1. Who We Are

Welcome to PixelForge (accessible via https://pixelsforge.cc). We provide a suite of free, high-performance web-based image and document utilities designed to operate natively within your web browser. If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or our data processing practices, please reach out via our dedicated Contact Us page.

2. Core Architecture & Local Processing (Zero Uploads)

Unlike traditional online converters that require users to upload sensitive files to remote cloud servers, PixelForge is engineered with a strict client-side processing architecture. When you resize, compress, crop, convert, or edit an image or PDF on PixelForge, all computational work occurs 100% locally within your device's web browser leveraging HTML5 Canvas, WebAssembly, and native JavaScript APIs.

Your uploaded files, documents, photos, and generated outputs are never transmitted over the internet, stored on any external server, or inspected by our administrators. Because files never leave your device, we maintain zero custody over your data.

3. Information We Collect

Because our core tools operate locally without user registration, we do not collect personal identifiers such as names, email addresses, or account credentials during normal tool usage. However, to maintain site operational health, analyze performance, and support free access via advertising, we and our service vendors collect limited non-personally identifiable technical information:

  • Log & Technical Data: Standard server logs may briefly capture anonymous request diagnostics, including browser type, operating system version, referring URL, timestamp, and anonymized IP address ranges.
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymized metrics regarding which specific utilities (e.g., Image Compression or PDF Merger) are loaded to help us prioritize engineering improvements.

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device to store site preferences and support third-party services. PixelForge uses essential functional cookies and third-party analytical and advertising cookies. You retain full control over cookie behavior via your browser's security preferences.

7. Your Statutory Privacy Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

European Union & EEA Residents (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), users located within the EEA, UK, or Switzerland hold rights regarding their personal data, including the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, data portability, and object to profiling. Because PixelForge processes files locally and does not store personal profiles, data requests can typically be verified as maintaining zero stored records on our servers.

California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California consumers have the right to request disclosure of categories of data collected, request deletion of personal records, and opt out of the sale or sharing of personal data. PixelForge does not sell personal information to third parties.

8. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

PixelForge is a professional utility platform designed for general audiences. Our website and services are not directed toward children under the age of 13 (or 16 in certain EEA jurisdictions). We do not knowingly solicit or collect personal information from minors.

9. Data Security

We enforce strict HTTPS encryption across all web pages to ensure secure delivery of our web application scripts. Coupled with our client-side execution model, your files are protected against transit interception.
